Our Story

I grew up in the countryside in Colombia and could still remember my experience as a child visiting my uncles, aunt and playing with my cousins at the “cafetales” (Spanish for coffee plantation) which were part of their daily livelihoods. Vivid in my mind are the odour of the cafetal, the sweet flavour of coffee cherries, and the sound of coffee beans being dried and stirred on the ground under the sun (common practice back then), and the smell of freshly roasted coffee beans.

Colombian coffee is all about a great cup, a nice conversation, with nice latin music in the background to complement the atmosphere to bring a great coffee experience.

We work directly with farmers in Colombia where we cooperate with each other to trial and share best practises to ensure the whole process is sustainable, and the quality is never compromised.

- Leidy Cuervo Rojas, Founder and Coffee Genius
