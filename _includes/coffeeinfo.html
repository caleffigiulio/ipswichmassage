Coffee Beans

Do you know how many tastes you can try in a cup of coffee? More than one, I can assure you!

The flavour notes on a cup of coffee are given by many parameters like: bean type (Arabica or Robusta), altitude, soil, coffee cherry processing (dry, wet, both), care given during transportation of the green bean, type of roasting (light, dark), and a skilled barista with the grinding tailored according to the brewing type (French press, pour over, espresso machine), and milk frothing.

About our beans taste notes:  Syrupy body, clean orange acidity, chocolate and sweet lingering finish providing a unique well balanced flavour. Curious to trying it out?! Swing by our Bike Coffee and not just feel the taste, but also the aroma and relax listening to Latin songs!

Tolima

Our coffee comes from Tolima, a city in the centre-west of Colombia.

Soil, temperature, altitude and solar radiation are elements that influences the coffee beans quality. Our farmers look after each one of these factors to provide you the best coffee experience!

When the best coffee beans are produced and honoured with the right roasting, the smell and nice dark colours of the bean are so beautiful that when in comes to grinding it brings a fundamental question..."To bean or not to bean?".

Água Panela

Do you know what Panela is like?!

It’s an unrefined dehydrated cane juice. Very popular in Colombia, you can have it as a hot or iced drink, or even adding some spoons in your regular coffee to get some extra flavour. The taste is all about a malty & caramel and subtle sweetness. Available in the Iced and hot versions.
