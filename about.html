Philosophy at Ipswich Massage and Herbal Spa follows a healthy living philosophy that takes its inspiration from nature.

We propose that tranquility need not to be found in any specific location but with the right tools, it can be discovered within oneself, by exploring body, mind and spirit.

We embrace lifelong learning and development, our company never wanting to be everything to everybody, but instead offering only the very best to an exacting few.

History

Ipswich Massage Center opened its doors in September 2009 at 11 Lawrence Street, North Ipswich with just two therapists.

By early 2010 we had added a third person to the team and hired a part time receptionist. In 2010 we also joined the HICCAPS program which enabled us to provide health insurance refunds.

2011 saw us moving to a bigger 5 room premises at Limestone Street Ipswich. The team now consisted of 5 therapists and two part time receptionists. We began 7 day trading and launched our online booking system.

In November 2012 Sonja Glinster bought the buildings on the corner of Waghorn and Brisbane Street with the vision of creating a health conscious hot spot for Ipswich. Demolition work began straight away, however, it was a long six months before the team at Ipswich Massage could move in. Mayor Paul Pisasle opened the new shop on 1st July with over 30 people attending the grand opening.

Services

Ipswich Massage specialises in hands-on healing combining traditional Eastern techniques with scientific advances. With our consultative, proactive approach, our knowledgeable experts recommend appropriate therapies and make suggestions according to your end pursuit, from lymphatic drainage to muscular pain to weight loss or anti-ageing skincare.

Always the service is intuitive, empathetic and compassionate, making you feel nurtured and supported every step of the way.But nor is the service we provide simply pampering; it is deeply knowledgeable, ranging from highly trained facialists to yoga teachers to Chinese doctors. Therapists are also cross-trained , thereby ensuring a high level of consistency and constantly refreshed expertise.
